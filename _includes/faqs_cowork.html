Frequently Asked Questions

By applying am I locked into anything?

No. We're don't collect payment details at this stage. You can withdraw your application at any time if you need to.

Is the Bootstrapper Membership open to small teams?

Yes, the membership is suitable for co-working teams of up to 3 people.

Where can I find out more about the co-working terms and conditions?

Terms and conditions are displayed as part of the membership sign up process.

How are applications assessed?

The assessment process reflects the Bootstrapper membership offering with criteria including technology fit, potential contribution to other Hub tenants and the talent development program as well as plans for growth.

How does payment work?

Memberships are billed monthly, in advance, via credit card.

Will there be other kinds of memberships?

We're launching with the Bootstrapper membership and will add other membership types in the coming month. Keep an eye on our Facebook page.

Are these rates in place forever?

These are introductory rates for inaugural members as we move through the foundation phase. The rate will be in place for the first 6 months and reviewed thereafter.
